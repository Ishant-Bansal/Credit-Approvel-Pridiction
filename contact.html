Frequently Asked Questions

How long does credit approval take?

Our AI-powered system provides instant decisions in under 60 seconds for most applications. Complex cases may take up to 24 hours.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade encryption and comply with all financial regulations including SOC 2 and PCI DSS standards to protect your data.

Are there any fees for using CreditFlow?

Checking your credit approval eligibility is completely free. We only charge fees if you proceed with a loan and they are clearly disclosed upfront.

Can I use CreditFlow on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can apply and manage your account from anywhere.

What credit score do I need?

We work with a wide range of credit scores. Our AI considers multiple factors beyond just credit score to provide fair assessments.

Can I reapply if I'm initially denied?

Yes, you can reapply after 30 days. We'll provide feedback on how to improve your chances for approval.
